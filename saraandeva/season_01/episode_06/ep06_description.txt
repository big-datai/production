Mama threw five small bagels in the backyard for the birds — but Ginger had OTHER ideas. One by one she carried them off, dug little holes by the petunias, and buried them with her nose. Meanwhile Sara was at the kitchen table reading "A New Pet" on the goreadling iPad — until they spotted Ginger through the window. Cue family detective mode: counting bumpies, reading plant-marker clues, and uncovering all five buried bagels. Plus: Eva FINALLY rides her two-wheel bike all by herself! 🚲

This is Episode 6 of Sara and Eva — a Pixar-style animated kids' show about two real-life sisters and their two real-life dogs.
