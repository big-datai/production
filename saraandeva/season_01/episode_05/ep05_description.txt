It's Sara's birthday — and waiting under the giant sparkly bow is the BIGGEST surprise yet: a three-floor pink-and-purple Dream House. But before Sara can play, Mama has one gentle question: "Will you share with your sister?" What follows is a warm sisters-share-everything adventure: Sara reads the tiny room labels (KITCHEN, BEDROOM), counts five little dolls, and figures out how to split them fairly between two girls. Ginger and Joe sniff their way in for some comic relief, and the episode lands on the lesson at the heart of it: when we share with the people we love, the joy gets bigger.

This is Episode 5 of Sara and Eva — a Pixar-style animated kids' show about two real-life sisters and their two real-life dogs.
