Halloween night! Sara and Eva go trick-or-treating in costume — Sara as a fairy princess, Eva as a tiny pumpkin, Papa as a friendly werewolf with wolf ears, Mama in a cozy autumn beanie, and Joe in his ladybug costume. But when the wind catches their porch's giant inflatable ghost, Ginger bolts down the sidewalk!

Now the family must visit five spooky-but-friendly Halloween houses on the block — the Witch Cauldron, the Pirate Ship, the Skeleton Lawn, Isabel's Sweet Cottage, and the Special Candy House — to find their lost dog. They meet new friends Lisa and the kindly Mrs. Patel along the way.

Will Joe help sniff out where Ginger is hiding? Watch and find out!

Featuring real recurring characters: Sara, Eva, Mama, Papa, Joe (Pomeranian), Ginger (Jack Russell), Isabel, and Leo.
