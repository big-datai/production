It's Mother's Day morning. Papa, Sara, and Eva have a SECRET PLAN: a perfect breakfast in bed for Mama. Pancakes, flowers, the whole deal. Eva tiptoes with the tray. Eva trips. The tray FLIES. Pancakes splat, juice everywhere, Ginger steals one mid-disaster. Eva bursts into tears: "I RUINED IT!" But Sara has a plan — a much better gift. A handmade coupon book full of TIME together: family day, dance party, story time, walking the puppies. Mama opens it, reads each coupon, and tears up: "I don't want a perfect breakfast. I want THIS — every single day with you."

This is Episode 7 of Sara and Eva — a Pixar-style animated kids' show about two real-life sisters and their two real-life dogs.
