Ginger steals the pancakes! Sara and Eva have to track down their sneaky Jack Russell before breakfast disappears. A morning of detective work, ABCs at the chalkboard, counting clues, and a big cuddly forgiveness hug — plus a peek at what Ginger's plotting for next week.
