# LYRICS

[Instrumental Intro — soft mysterious piano + light strings]

[Verse — gentle whispered narrator voice, child-friendly]
Tip-toe, tip-toe, in the moonlight glow,
A little paw is sneaking where no one knows.
Quiet as a feather, soft as a sigh,
A puppy on a mission, with magic in his eye.

[Pre-Chorus — building soft suspense]
Where did the tooth go? Where could it be?
Look behind the couch, look under the tree,
A little furry secret, a hidden surprise,
Joe is keeping treasures right beneath our eyes.

[Chorus — soft, mysterious, kid-friendly wonder]
Mystery, mystery, what will we find?
A pancake, a key, a treasure of every kind!
Pad pad pad, soft and slow,
Tooth Fairy is watching, but only Joe knows.

[Verse 2 — slightly playful, still hushed]
Through the silent house where the night-lights gleam,
A little puppy moves like he's in a dream.
Stash and a wish and a coin to keep,
While the whole house sleeps and the stars shine deep.

[Outro — soft mysterious resolve]
Mystery, mystery, what will we find?
A puppy and a heart that's full and kind.
Tip-toe, tip-toe, the moon says goodnight,
A secret little story, soft and bright.

# GENRE

Soft mysterious children's lullaby, hushed and curious. Light strings, pizzicato plucks, soft piano, gentle wood-block percussion, occasional twinkle bells. Tempo around 75 BPM, key A-minor or D-minor. Whisper-soft vocals from a single warm female voice (storyteller-style), no shouting, no high-energy beats. Mood: bedtime mystery — like the underscore for a Bluey or Bing episode when something hidden is about to be discovered. Avoid: synth, EDM, drums above mp dynamic, dance beats, autotune. Atmosphere should feel cozy-suspenseful, never scary. 1:30 runtime so it can loop softly under 45 seconds of silent narrative clips.
