Mama, Papa, sister, brother of love.

[Outro — soft]
We love you, Mama. We love you, Papa.
Happy anniversary.

# GENRE

Children's musical anniversary love song. Soft acoustic piano (gentle intro), warm acoustic guitar, light glockenspiel sparkles on chorus. Lead vocal: 6-year-old girl voice (sweet, slightly nasal, like Sara), joined by 3-year-old girl voice (toddler-squeaky, like Eva) on chorus. Backup: warm mom-voiced soft harmony for fill. Tempo 92 BPM, key of C major, 4/4 time. Production aesthetic: Bluey theme + Pixar Up love-story instrumental + Mister Rogers warmth. Light strings on bridge. Final chorus has children-choir feel with both girls together. NO drum kit, NO synth bass, NO electric guitar, NO heavy production. Cozy-family-evening atmosphere, soft analog warmth, room-tone reverb. Total length 2:00 (120s) — this is critical for the music block visual loop sync.
