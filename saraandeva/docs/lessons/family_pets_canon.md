The Sara & Eva family has **two dogs only**:

- **Joe** — male, 9 years old, Pomeranian, **Eva's dog**. Family pet from ep01 ("The Puppies Want Pancakes"). Channel-hero in ep04 ("Joe's Secret Stash"). Slow-mo burger heister in ep11.
- **Ginger** — female, 4 years old, **Jack Russell terrier**, family dog. Family pet from ep01. Pancake thief in ep02, package mystery in ep03, bagel burglar in ep06.

**Why:** User explicitly corrected this 2026-05-06 after I (a) invented a non-canonical "Mike" character claiming Joe was Mike's neighbor dog and (b) carried over an old memory note that treated ep10/ep12's "Ginger" as a separate magic-forest fox character. Both wrong. Cost two rejected pitches in the ep15 brainstorm.

**How to apply:**
- Joe = Eva's dog. Always living at the family home. NOT owned by any neighbor / Mike / outside character. Costume preferences in story decisions: bug, hot-dog, dragon — anything cute, never scary.
- Ginger = the family Jack Russell. Always living at the family home. White body with brown patches on ears + back, short legs, alert ears.
- **ep10 and ep12 references to "Ginger" mean THIS Jack Russell — the same family dog.** ep12's logline framing of "Ginger as a magic-forest fox" is a wrong-canon gloss; the in-story note in the bottle was from the family dog Ginger having had a magic-forest adventure, not from a separate fox character. Future eps continuing the magic-forest arc (ep16, ep20) should treat Ginger-the-family-dog as the through-line, not invent a parallel forest character.
- The "magic deer" the family met in ep10 IS a separate creature (briefly seen, no name) — that one stays distinct from Ginger.
- Ages matter for casting: Joe-as-9yo reads more dignified-and-unbothered (good for slow-mo comedy heists). Ginger-as-4yo reads more zoomy / spookable / playful (good for chase + spook beats like ep15's escape scene).
