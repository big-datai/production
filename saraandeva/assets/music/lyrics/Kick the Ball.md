# Kick the Ball

**Genre:** Upbeat children's pop / soccer-cheer anthem
**Tempo:** ~135 BPM
**Mood:** Confident, playful, energetic — Eva's overconfident-soccer hook from ep10
**Voicing:** Two young girl voices (call-and-response). Bright, cheerful, playful.
**Length target:** ~60 seconds

---

## Notes for Suno

- Keep the chorus repetition tight and punchy — "kick the ball" three times in a row is the hook.
- Sister-rivalry energy in the call-and-response (Eva proud, Sara dryly skeptical).
- No instruments that sound "ominous" — keep it bright pop with claps + kid choir feel.
- Layer kid handclaps on every "kick" for percussion.
