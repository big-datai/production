# Can't Catch Us!

**Genre:** Playful kids' chase-tag pop / Papa-tag music video
**Tempo:** ~140 BPM (energetic)
**Mood:** Joyful chaos, kid-defies-parent comedy, playground tag-game
**Voicing:** Two young girl voices (Sara + Eva, lots of unison shouting "YOU'RE IT!") + Papa booming in low voice "I'M COMING!" — call-and-response between the kids and Papa
**Length target:** ~60 seconds

## Notes for Suno

- HIGH ENERGY playground-tag song. Rapid clapping percussion, bouncy ukulele or mandolin, light tambourine.
- Lots of UNISON kid shouts ("YOU'RE IT!", "CAN'T CATCH US!"). They should sound 5-7 years old, gleefully shouting.
- Papa's voice is layered in occasionally — DEEP and BOOMING but obviously playful, mock-monster style. Not actually scary.
- Add cartoon SFX: a "BOING!" when Papa "pops" out of the slide, kid giggle-clusters, faint playground-creak sounds.
- The song is designed to LOOP cleanly — 60s and Suno can extend with repeat-instrumental if needed.
- Energy stays HIGH the whole time. No quiet moments except the bridge "I see you..." mock-monster bit.
