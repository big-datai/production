# Tomorrow's Adventure

**Genre:** Twilight wonder ballad / sister-bond closer
**Tempo:** ~95 BPM (medium-slow, warm)
**Mood:** Dreamy, hopeful, sister-co-conspirators-with-a-plan
**Voicing:** Two young girl voices (sister duet). Soft acoustic-guitar-led arrangement. Warm and intimate.
**Length target:** ~60 seconds

---

## Lyrics

**[Intro — soft acoustic guitar fingerpicking + warm synth pad, 4 bars]**

**[Verse 1 — Sara, soft]**
Drew a map across the page,
Sketched an island in the haze,
Drew an X to mark the spot
Where a little fox is got.

**[Verse 2 — Eva, hushed]**
Drew a boat with a tiny sail,
Drew a star to make us prevail,
Closed my eyes and I could see
Where tomorrow's gonna lead me.

**[Chorus — both, in harmony]**
Tomorrow's adventure starts tonight,
Pencil-drawn under the sunset light.
Tomorrow we will sail away,
Find a friend and save the day.

**[Verse 3 — Sara]**
Phase one done, the base is built,
Sand stuck on my chin and a smile to tilt,
Phase two next we figure out the boat,
Wood and rope to keep us all afloat.

**[Pre-Chorus — Eva]**
You and me and Mama too,
Daddy with a big canoe,
Fox in trouble, fox to save,
Sister captains brave!

**[Chorus — both, slightly bigger]**
Tomorrow's adventure starts tonight,
Pencil-drawn under the sunset light.
Tomorrow we will sail away,
Find a friend and save the day.

**[Bridge — both, dreamy]**
Stars above the sand,
Plan inside our hand,
Hold tight little fox —
Tomorrow we set sail.

**[Final Chorus — both, harmony with reverb-tail]**
Tomorrow's adventure starts tonight,
Pencil-drawn under the sunset light.
Tomorrow we will sail away,

**[Outro — guitar fingerpicking fade, single seagull-call SFX, soft surf]**

---

## Notes for Suno

- Acoustic guitar fingerpicking is the core. Warm synth pad underneath. Subtle shaker for percussion.
- This is a CLOSER song — plays as the sun fully sets and Sara writes the rescue plan in her notebook.
- Sister harmony — they sing the chorus together. Verse 1 = Sara solo, Verse 2 = Eva solo, then together for the rest.
- Slow but not sleepy. Hopeful and dreamy.
- Layer light seagull-call and gentle surf SFX low in the mix.
