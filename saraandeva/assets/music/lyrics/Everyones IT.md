# Everyone's IT!

**Genre:** Bouncy kid-pop / friendship-celebration anthem
**Tempo:** ~140 BPM (high-energy, sprint-able)
**Mood:** Playground tag-game in full swing — Sara, Eva, Papa, Isabel, little-brother Leo, and 3 friend kids ALL playing tag together. Friendship-making energy at peak.

**Voicing:**
- **Sara + Eva (co-leads)** = high-energy kid voices, calling out the chorus together
- **Papa (warm playful backing)** = "I'M COMING!" / "GOTCHA!" call-outs, warm silly-monster energy (NOT scary)

**Length target:** 60 seconds (1 minute — fills the music-video block looped 6× over a 10s Kling Nano-Banana-pre-rendered group-tag clip).

## Notes for Suno

- **CRITICAL voicing**: Sara + Eva are HIGH-ENERGY kid co-leads. Group chorus is a STACK of mixed-age kid voices for the "EVERYONE'S IT!" repeats. Papa's backing is WARM and PLAYFUL, NEVER scary or commanding.
- **Mood**: bouncy playground anthem — like Cocomelon's tag songs but with more 50s-doo-wop polish.
- **Voicing tags for Suno**: `bouncy kid pop`, `mixed kid chorus`, `boy-girl-girl-group`, `warm dad backing`, `friendship anthem`, `playground tag song`, `high-energy`, `~140 BPM`. Avoid: `aggressive`, `EDM drops`, `trap drums`.
- **Instrumentation**: bouncy ukulele on 2 and 4, kid-friendly hand-claps stacked on the chorus, brushed snare, walking bass, light xylophone runs between verse and chorus. Kid voices stack thick on the "EVERYONE'S IT!" hook.
- **SFX layered**: kid laughter throughout, distant playground sounds (swings creaking softly), running-feet softs, hand-clap stack on every chorus.
- **Energy**: PEAK high throughout — never let it dip. Even the bridge stays bouncy. Final chorus is the loudest moment.
- **DO NOT**: aggressive trap-pop, EDM drops, ballad slow-down, autotune, scary-sounding tag-monster Papa.
