# Push Me Higher!

**Genre:** Soft kid-pop / wonder-ballad lullaby (slow-mo swing dreaminess)
**Tempo:** ~75 BPM (slow + dreamy — matches slow-motion swing footage)
**Mood:** Pure father-daughter wonder. Eva on the swing flying gently in slow-motion, Papa pushing her with warm dad-cheer, golden-hour light.

**Voicing:**
- **Eva (lead)** = soft little-girl wonder-voice. Singing dreamily, awe-struck, sing-song.
- **Papa (warm dad backing)** = warm, soft, low-key dad-voice. Sings the "hold on, little astronaut" responses gently.
- Background: glockenspiel + airy strings + gentle flute, like Pink Tree Whisper (ep10) tonally.

**Length target:** 60 seconds (1 minute — fills the music-video block looped 6× over a 10s slow-mo Kling render).

## Notes for Suno

- **CRITICAL voicing**: Eva is a SOFT, AWE-FILLED little-girl voice. NOT belt-y, NOT power-vocal. Dreamy and gentle.
- **Mood**: pastel-Pixar wonder beat. Pink Tree Whisper (ep10) tonally — same slow-motion magic-discovery feeling.
- **Voicing tags for Suno**: `soft kid wonder vocal`, `dreamy lullaby`, `warm dad backing`, `glockenspiel`, `airy strings`, `slow tempo`, `Pixar-style wonder`. Avoid: `belt`, `power vocal`, `up-tempo`, `pop-rock`.
- **Instrumentation**: soft glockenspiel arpeggios, airy strings, gentle flute, very soft brushed snare on the chorus only, no heavy drums, plenty of reverb.
- **SFX layered**: soft "whoosh" of swing motion under each chorus, distant playground laughter very low in mix, soft wind chime at the start and end.
- **Energy**: stays SOFT and DREAMY throughout. Even the "highest" chorus is more glittering than belt-y.
- **DO NOT**: pop-rock energy, heavy drums, fast tempo, autotune, dance-beat. This is a wonder-beat ballad.
