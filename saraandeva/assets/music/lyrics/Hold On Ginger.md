# LYRICS

[Verse 1 — Sara, determined and warm]
Found a bottle on the shore today,
Tiny pawprint on the page.
"Ginger here, I'm far from home" —
Little dog, you're not alone.

[Pre-Chorus — Sara]
Pack the cooler, grab the map,
Sister sister, we won't nap!

[Chorus — both girls + Mama+Papa warm "yeah, yeah" backing]
HOLD ON, GINGER —
WE'RE COMING TO YOU!
By the moon, by the stars,
By the stripes on the blue!
Through the surf, through the sand,
Build a boat, take a stand —
HOLD ON, GINGER —
WE'RE COMING TO YOU!

[Verse 2 — Eva, sweet and small but brave]
Got my flashlight, got my plan,
Got my sister, got my dad and mom.
We'll cross the water, cross the night,
You'll see our flashlight shining bright.

[Bridge — Eva → both]
We love you, Ginger —
You're our dog, you're our friend —
Hold on tight, we'll be there,
And we'll bring you home in the end!

[Final Chorus — big]
HOLD ON, GINGER —
WE'RE COMING TO YOU!

[Outro — both, soft tag]
…hold on, Ginger…
…we're coming to you…

# GENRE

Kids' adventure pop, Bluey-style hopeful family rescue anthem. ~118 BPM, hopeful + brave (NOT sad). Two young girl lead voices — Sara (older, clearer) on V1 + chorus, Eva (younger, sweeter) on V2 + bridge. Warm Mama+Papa backing on chorus refrain only ("yeah, yeah"). Acoustic guitar fingerpick + tambourine + soft hand-claps on 2+4 + gentle "ooh" group backups. Twilight tone. Promise voice — never sad. ~60 sec.
