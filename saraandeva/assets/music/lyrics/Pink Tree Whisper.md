# Pink Tree Whisper

**Genre:** Soft children's lullaby / awe ballad
**Tempo:** ~75 BPM (slow, dreamy)
**Mood:** Wonder, discovery, gentle awe — Eva's solo at the magic-creature pink-hollow tree
**Voicing:** One young girl solo voice, soft and breathy. Whispered verses, sung chorus. Optional faint sister harmony in chorus.
**Length target:** ~60 seconds

---

## Lyrics

**[Intro — soft glockenspiel + airy strings, 4 bars]**

**[Verse 1 — whispered]**
Tip-toe past the tall pine trees,
Listen to the magic breeze —
Something pink between the leaves,
What could possibly be?

**[Pre-Chorus]**
A tiny door in the tree-bark wall,
Painted pink and oh so small...
Are they sleeping? Are they near?
Do they know that we are here?

**[Chorus — sung, gentle]**
Pink tree, pink tree, what do you keep?
Magic creatures fast asleep.
Pink tree, pink tree, what do you hide?
Tiny lanterns glowing inside.

**[Verse 2 — whispered]**
Fireflies float on the pollen-light,
Forest's holding its breath so tight —
Maybe if we stand SO STILL,
We'll see one, we will, we will...

**[Bridge — almost spoken]**
Don't scare them...
Don't make a sound...
Just listen
to the ground...

**[Final Chorus — gentle, with optional second voice harmony]**
Pink tree, pink tree, what do you keep?
Magic creatures fast asleep.
Pink tree, pink tree, sleep till spring —
We'll come back and bring you everything.

**[Outro — single firefly sound, soft echo of "pink tree..." fading out]**

---

## Notes for Suno

- This song plays under the slow-mo Eva-solo music video at the pink-hollow tree.
- Keep the chorus melody simple and sing-along-able — kids will hum it for days.
- No big drums. Glockenspiel, soft strings, light piano, maybe one airy synth pad.
- Whispered verses contrast with the gentle sung chorus to feel like she's discovering something secret.
- Tempo and energy are deliberately low — the song should feel like a shared secret, not a banger.
