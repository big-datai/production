# Slow-Mo Burger Heist

**Genre**: Surf-rock heist instrumental with a sprinkle of jazzy 60s spy-noir
**Tempo**: 92 BPM (mid-slow groove — feels heroic and cheeky in slow-mo)
**Mood**: Comically epic — the canine equivalent of an Ocean's Eleven montage. Bass-line walking surf rhythm, jazzy wah-wah guitar, a single mischievous flute, hand-claps on the 2 and 4. Light dog-yip ad-libs.
**Voicing**: 70% INSTRUMENTAL. The vocalist is a single playful narrator — like a kid play-acting a heist movie. The two girls (Sara + Eva) call-and-respond from the background like co-conspirators.
**Length target**: 60s
**Notes for Suno**: keep the feel CHEEKY-COMIC, not actually serious crime music — think "The Pink Panther theme has a sleepover with Wallace and Gromit." NO autotune, no trap. Surf-rock guitar, walking bass, one playful flute. Dog yip ad-libs allowed. KEEP IT KID-FRIENDLY — no gritty undertones.
