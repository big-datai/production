# World's Okayest Dad

**Genre**: 50s sock-hop / dad-rock celebration (early Beach Boys + Phineas-and-Ferb dad-energy)
**Tempo**: 128 BPM (peppy, upbeat, hand-claps territory)
**Mood**: Goofy proud dad-celebration. Hand-claps, cheerful brass-stab punctuation, doo-wop background "ba-da-da" chorus. Pure family-comedy joy.
**Voicing**: A single warm playful adult-male lead voice (Papa-ish singing to himself). The two girls (Sara + Eva) jump in on the chorus shouting "GO PAPA GO!" call-and-response.
**Length target**: 30s
**Notes for Suno**: KEEP IT WARM AND WHOLESOME — Phineas-and-Ferb dad-songs are the reference. No autotune. Doo-wop "ba-da-da" backups. Hand-claps on 2 and 4. Brief brass-stab on title repeats. Kids' shouts on chorus only.

---

(BEAT — 0:00–0:02 — hand-claps + doo-wop "ba-da-da-da" intro)

**(VERSE — Papa-voice lead, warm playful)**

I'm not the BEST and I'm not the WORST
I burn the grill but I do it FIRST
The pup just stole the WHOLE DARN spread
But I'm OKAYEST — that's what my apron said!

(GIRLS, shouting back): *GO PAPA GO!*

**(CHORUS — full band, brass-stab on title)**

I'm the WORLD'S OKAYEST DAD! (BAM!)
(GIRLS: BA-DA-DA-DA!)
With a charcoal-colored apron and a SPATULA in hand!
(GIRLS: GO! GO! GO!)
Round-one's gone and round-two's gone TOO
But round-three's coming and I'm KEEPING ONE EYE on you!
(YOU! YOU! YOU!)

(BEAT — 0:18 — eight-bar instrumental break — doo-wop backups, hand-claps, walking bass)

**(TAG — Papa-voice, half-spoken, cheerful)**

Pomeranian, pomeranian, you can't HIDE…
Papa's keeping ONE eye open WIIIIIIDE…

**(FINAL HIT — full band, kids cheering)**

WORLD'S OKAYEST DAD!
(WORLD'S OKAYEST DAD! GO PAPA GO!)
WORLD'S OKAYEST DAD!
(BAM!)

(BEAT — outro — single hand-clap, cheerful dad chuckle, dog yip "Mff!")
