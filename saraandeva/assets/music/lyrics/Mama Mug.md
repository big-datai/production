# Mama Mug

**Theme:** Heart-line slow-mo. Mama sips the painted mug Eva made. Quiet, tender, hand-painted hearts.
**Suggested Suno style:** soft acoustic ballad, fingerpicked guitar + warm strings + breathy female vocal, BPM ~70, major key, very quiet first 4 bars.
**Pairs with:** [B.json](../../content/episodes/ep09/B.json) (Mama solo heart-slow-mo render).

---

[Intro — soft fingerpicked guitar]
(instrumental 4 bars)

[Verse 1 — soft, almost spoken]
Little hands paint a tiny heart,
Tiny heart for a tired mama,
Steam rises through the painted hearts,
And the world goes quiet for a minute.

[Chorus]
You are my coffee, you are my morning,
You are the warmth in the painted mug,
Tiny hearts hand-painted for me,
Tiny hands holding all of my love.

[Verse 2]
Empty shelf became a story,
Story of two little planners,
Whipped cream tower in a cup of love,
And a tear falls slow into wonder.

[Chorus repeat]
You are my coffee, you are my morning,
You are the warmth in the painted mug,
Tiny hearts hand-painted for me,
Tiny hands holding all of my love.

[Outro — voice trails, guitar fade]
Tiny hands... tiny hearts... tiny mug...
You are my coffee, my morning, my love.
