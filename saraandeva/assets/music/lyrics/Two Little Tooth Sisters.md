# Two Little Tooth Sisters

**Theme:** Sara and Eva going to the dentist together — bouncy, sing-along, sister duo dance.
**Suggested Suno style:** upbeat kid-pop, ukulele + claps + bright synth, BPM ~110, female child voices (or pitched-up vocal), major key.
**Pairs with:** [A.json](../../content/episodes/ep08/A.json) (Sara + Eva duo dance music video).
