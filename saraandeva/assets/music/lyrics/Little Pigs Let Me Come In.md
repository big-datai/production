# Little Pigs Let Me Come In!

**Genre:** Doo-wop kids' comedy / cartoon-wolf duet (warm + silly, NOT scary)
**Tempo:** ~135 BPM (bouncy march-step rhythm so Papa-as-wolf can walk in time)
**Mood:** Classic Three Little Pigs role-play played for laughs — Papa is the silly Big Bad Wolf who keeps getting locked out, kids are the giggly little-pig sisters.

**Voicing:** TWO clearly contrasting voices:
- **Papa = WARM CARTOON WOLF VOICE** — silly, mock-dramatic, NEVER scary or growly. Think Goofy-as-wolf or Looney-Tunes friendly-monster. Falls into theatrical disappointed slumps every chorus.
- **Sara + Eva = GIGGLY LITTLE-GIRL PIG SISTERS** — sing-songy, refusing playfully, hand-on-hip sass.

**Length target:** 120 seconds (2 minutes — fills the music-video block looped 8× over a 15s base render).

## Notes for Suno

- **CRITICAL voicing**: Papa is a SILLY, WARM, comedic CARTOON WOLF. NOT a scary growly wolf. NEVER deep-bass-villain. Kid-show-friendly, like Looney Tunes or Disney's friendly-wolf cameos.
- **Mood**: 1950s doo-wop / sock-hop / Tex Avery cartoon-fairy-tale energy. Think "Mr. Sandman" meets "Three Little Pigs at recess."
- **Voicing tags for Suno**: `cartoon wolf vocals`, `friendly comedic wolf`, `little-girl pig duet`, `boy-girl-girl trio`, `Beach-Boys-style backing`, `vaudeville comedy duet`, `kids-show fairy-tale`. Avoid: any growl, any growl-bass, any horror-wolf vocal.
- **Instrumentation**: walking upright bass on quarter notes (matches Papa's tippy-toe walk around tower), brushed snare on 2 and 4, kazoo + clarinet for the wolf's theatrical wind-up, sing-song chorus harmonies for the pigs.
- **SFX layered**: cartoon knock-knock-knock SFX between verse/chorus, gentle "huff" and "puff" wind SFX in the bridge, kid giggles throughout, soft "boi-oi-oing" cartoon outro.
- **Energy**: warm + bouncy throughout. Papa's slumps are theatrical, not actually sad. NEVER let it go scary.
- **DO NOT**: deep growly wolf, scary fairy-tale-villain wolf, modern trap drums, autotune, slow-ballad sections, environmental shake (no thunder), "actually blowing the house down" sound (it's just Papa's gentle puff).
