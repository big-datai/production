## 🎨 STYLES — paste into Suno's "Styles" field (≤ 1000 chars)

```
1950s doo-wop kids fairy-tale comedy, sock-hop swing rhythm, ~135 BPM, bouncy march-step. Two clearly contrasting voices: warm comedic CARTOON WOLF (Papa) — silly Goofy/Looney-Tunes friendly-wolf, theatrical disappointed slumps, NEVER scary or growly; plus GIGGLY LITTLE-GIRL PIG SISTERS (Sara + Eva) — sing-songy refusal, hand-on-hip sass, mixed kid duet. Walking upright bass quarter notes, brushed snare on 2 and 4, kazoo + clarinet during the wolf wind-up, piano-triplet stings, doo-wop "shoo-bop" backing harmonies. Cartoon SFX layered: knock-knock between verse-and-chorus, gentle huff-and-puff wind in the bridge, kid giggles throughout, soft "boi-oi-oing" cartoon outro. Energy stays bouncy + warm + theatrical — even Papa's slumps are comedic, not actually sad. AVOID: deep growly bass-villain wolf, scary fairy-tale-villain, modern trap drums, autotune, slow ballad sections, environmental shake/thunder, real-house-blowing-down sound. Length target ~120 seconds.
```

## How to use

1. Open suno.com/create → Custom mode
2. Paste LYRICS block into the Lyrics field
3. Paste STYLES block into the Styles field
4. Generate. Expect 3–5 attempts to land BOTH the warm cartoon-wolf Papa AND the giggly-pig sisters duet
5. If Papa's voice renders as scary/growly, regenerate (sometimes Suno over-indexes on "wolf")
6. Save mp3 as `assets/music/Little Pigs Let Me Come In.mp3`

ep13 music-video block A: 15s Kling render of Papa pacing around the playground tower, looped 8× to fill this 120s song.
