# Two Dogs One Dream

**Genre:** Doo-wop kids' comedy / dog-duet vaudeville
**Tempo:** ~135 BPM (bouncy sock-hop)
**Mood:** Mischievous canine glee, total dog-confidence, 50s doo-wop pastiche played for laughs

**Voicing — TWO CONTRASTING CARTOON DOG VOICES, ONE GIRL + ONE BOY:**
- **Ginger = CARTOON GIRL-DOG VOICE.** Higher pitch, peppy, sassy, yappy, brighter timbre. Think a sassy little-girl cartoon dog (Disney sidekick / Tex-Avery girly mutt). MUST always read as clearly FEMININE.
- **Joe = CARTOON BOY-DOG VOICE.** Lower pitch, rounder, floofy-doofy, charming-but-clumsy big-boy energy. Think a sweet boy cartoon dog (Goofy's nephew / Looney-Tunes boy mutt). MUST always read as clearly MASCULINE.
- Call-and-response alternates GIRL → BOY → GIRL → BOY. Listener should always know which dog is singing.
- Backup chorus: mixed boy-and-girl cartoon-dog "shoo-bop shoo-bop" stack, Beach-Boys-style.

**Length target:** ~60 seconds

## Notes for Suno

- **CRITICAL — gender voicing must be obvious:** Ginger = CARTOON GIRL-DOG (feminine, higher, sassy). Joe = CARTOON BOY-DOG (masculine, lower, big-boy floofy). If Suno renders both as the same gender, REGENERATE. The contrast is the whole gag.
- **Mood**: 1950s doo-wop / sock-hop / Tex Avery cartoon energy — "Mr. Sandman" meets a Looney Tunes dog short.
- **Voicing tags for Suno**: `cartoon girl-dog lead vocal`, `cartoon boy-dog lead vocal`, `boy-girl dog duo`, `Beach-Boys-style mixed-gender backing`, `vaudeville comedy duet`. Avoid same-voice doo-wop.
- **Instrumentation**: walking upright double bass on quarter notes, brushed snare on 2 and 4, saxophone wails on the chorus drops, doo-wop "shoo-bop" backup vocals throughout, piano-triplet stings during the chorus.
- **SFX layered**: cartoon dog "WOOF!" stings between verses, single burp SFX at the very end, light paw-tap percussion.
- **Energy**: never let the energy drop below 80%. Keep it bouncy and silly the whole way.
- **DO NOT**: autotune (kills the cartoon-dog vibe), modern trap drums, slow ballad sections, serious vocals, both voices same gender (must be girl + boy duo).
- **This is the companion piece** to ep11's "Slow-Mo Burger Heist" (Joe solo) — same heist, dogs' POV, full doo-wop celebration.
