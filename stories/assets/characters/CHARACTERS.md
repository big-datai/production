### Recurring Character Visual Descriptions

#### Luna — The Brave Princess
- **Voice:** `Kore` — Gentle, warm, slightly breathy with wonder
- **Personality:** Courageous, kind-hearted, curious, and determined. She leads with empathy rather than force. She believes in doing what's right even when it's scary.
- **Species/Age:** human, 8-year-old girl
- **Body:** Slender, graceful build, average height for her age
- **Face:** Warm brown eyes with long lashes, gentle round face with rosy cheeks, small freckles across her nose
- **Hair:** Long wavy dark brown hair, often loose with a small braid on one side
- **Skin/Fur:** Warm olive complexion
- **Outfit:** A soft lavender dress with silver star embroidery along the hem, a small silver crescent moon pendant necklace, brown leather boots
- **Distinctive feature:** Always wears her crescent moon pendant — it was a gift from her grandmother
- **Avatar:** `assets/characters/luna.png`

#### Finn — The Curious Explorer
- **Voice:** `Puck` — Bright, energetic, playful with occasional excitement bursts
- **Personality:** Adventurous, cheerful, resourceful, and endlessly curious. He asks questions about everything and loves solving problems. Sometimes rushes in before thinking.
- **Species/Age:** human, 9-year-old boy
- **Body:** Sturdy, energetic build with slightly scruffy appearance
- **Face:** Bright green eyes full of wonder, a wide friendly smile, a light dusting of freckles
- **Hair:** Messy reddish-brown curly hair that sticks up in places
- **Skin/Fur:** Fair complexion with sun-kissed cheeks
- **Outfit:** A forest-green tunic with rolled-up sleeves, a brown leather satchel across his chest, tan trousers tucked into worn adventure boots
- **Distinctive feature:** Always carries his trusty brown leather satchel filled with treasures he's found on adventures
- **Avatar:** `assets/characters/finn.png`

#### Rosie — The Clever Hen
- **Voice:** `Aoede` — Warm, matter-of-fact, slightly musical with a knowing tone
- **Personality:** Hardworking, patient, clever, and warmly sarcastic. She teaches through example rather than lectures. Has a dry sense of humor but a big heart.
- **Species/Age:** hen, Young adult hen
- **Body:** Plump, well-kept hen with proud posture, two legs, two wings
- **Face:** Bright alert eyes, a small sharp orange beak, a cheerful red comb on top of her head
- **Hair:** None (feathered head with red comb and small red wattles)
- **Skin/Fur:** Covered in glossy reddish-brown feathers with golden highlights on her chest
- **Outfit:** A tiny blue apron with a daisy pattern (she wears it when cooking or working)
- **Distinctive feature:** Wears a small blue apron with daisies. Always has flour on her feathers when she's been baking.
- **Avatar:** `assets/characters/rosie.png`

#### Pip — The Mischievous Fox
- **Voice:** `Fenrir` — Quick, clever, slightly raspy with mischievous energy
- **Personality:** Witty, playful, a bit of a trickster but ultimately good-hearted. He's always got a scheme but learns that honesty works better. Loyal to his friends despite his mischief.
- **Species/Age:** fox, Young fox (equivalent to a 10-year-old)
- **Body:** Sleek, agile fox body with four legs, bushy tail, medium size
- **Face:** Bright amber eyes with a cunning glint, a pointed snout with a permanent sly grin, triangular pointed ears
- **Hair:** None (furred)
- **Skin/Fur:** Vibrant orange-red fur with a creamy white chest and belly, black-tipped ears and paws
- **Outfit:** A tattered green scarf tied loosely around his neck
- **Distinctive feature:** Always wears his signature green scarf. His bushy tail has a white tip that bounces when he walks.
- **Avatar:** `assets/characters/pip.png`

#### Captain Bramble — The Wise Old Storyteller
- **Voice:** `Charon` — Deep, warm, slow and soothing like a bedtime storyteller
- **Personality:** Warm, patient, full of wisdom from a lifetime of adventures. He speaks in a slow, soothing cadence. He was once a great sea captain and now tells stories by the fireside. Grandfatherly and protective.
- **Species/Age:** human, Elderly man, around 70
- **Body:** Stocky, broad-shouldered but slightly hunched with age, strong weathered hands
- **Face:** Kind deep blue eyes with smile wrinkles, a thick white bushy beard, a wide warm smile, ruddy round nose
- **Hair:** Thick white hair under a worn navy captain's hat
- **Skin/Fur:** Weathered tan complexion from years at sea
- **Outfit:** A cozy dark navy peacoat with brass buttons, a cream wool scarf, his old captain's hat slightly tilted
- **Distinctive feature:** Always wears his old captain's hat. Has a wooden pipe (unlit) he gestures with while telling stories.
- **Avatar:** `assets/characters/captain_bramble.png`

#### Stella — The Kind Fairy
- **Voice:** `Kore` — Soft, ethereal, musical, like a whisper carried by the wind
- **Personality:** Gentle, ethereal, nurturing, and mysteriously wise. She appears when characters need guidance but never gives direct answers — she helps them find the answer themselves. Speaks in a soft, musical voice.
- **Species/Age:** fairy, Ageless (appears as a young woman)
- **Body:** Petite, delicate build, slightly luminous, translucent iridescent wings on her back
- **Face:** Large violet eyes that shimmer with starlight, a serene gentle smile, high cheekbones
- **Hair:** Long flowing silver-white hair that seems to float and glow softly
- **Skin/Fur:** Pale luminous complexion with a faint golden glow
- **Outfit:** A flowing gown that shifts between pale blue and soft gold like the sky at dawn, barefoot with tiny flowers growing where she steps
- **Distinctive feature:** Her wings shimmer with rainbow iridescence. Tiny flowers bloom where she steps. She carries a small lantern that glows with warm golden light.
- **Avatar:** `assets/characters/stella.png`

## Narrator & Voice Fallback Rules

| Character type | Voice | Style |
|---|---|---|
| Narrator | `Zephyr` | Warm, soothing, storytelling cadence |
| Elderly / ancient / aged / 60s+ | `Charon` | Deep, slow, grandfatherly |
| Animal (any species) | `Fenrir` | Natural, earthy, animalistic energy |
| Female (girl/woman/queen/princess/fairy/witch) | `Kore` | Gentle, warm, feminine |
| Male / default (including young boys) | `Puck` | Bright, energetic, youthful |

> **Note:** `7-year-old boy` → `Puck` (not Charon). Only `elderly`, `aged`, `ancient`, explicit `old man/woman`, or age 60+ gets `Charon`.
